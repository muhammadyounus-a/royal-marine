Services

We provide a wide range of upholstery services in Doha, Qatar. Our skilled craftsmen are experts
in renovating old furniture into modern. Some of the services are listed below.

Custom & Upholstery

Tailor your furniture to perfection with our custom upholstery service. Choose from a diverse range of high-quality fabrics and materials to create a unique and personalized look that suits your style.

Furniture Restoration

Breathe new life into cherished pieces withour furniture restoration service. Ourskilled artisans meticulously repair and refurbish,preserving the integrity and character of your furniture.

Foam Replacement

Enhance the comfort and longevity of your furniture with our foam replacement service. We expertly replace worn-out or damaged foam, restoring your seating to its original plushness.

Antique Upholstery

Preserve the charm of antique furniture with our specialized antique upholstery service. Our craftsmen are adept at maintaining the authenticity of historical pieces while ensuring durability.

Leather & Upholstery

Indulge in luxury with our leather upholstery service. Elevate your furniture with the timeless appeal of leather, expertly crafted and tailored to enhance both aesthetics and durability.

Reupholstery Services

Give a fresh, contemporary look to your furniture with our reupholstery service. Whether updating worn-out fabric or embracing a new style, we transform your pieces to meet the latest trends

Cushion Replacement

Revitalize your seating experience by opting for our cushion replacement service. Say goodbye to sagging cushions and welcome enhanced support and comfort to your furniture.

Commercial Upholstery

Elevate the ambiance of your business space with our commercial upholstery service. From office furniture to restaurant seating, we combine style and durability to create a lasting impression on your clients and customers.

Royal Marine Upholstery specializes in expert stitching work for both marine vessels and furniture, combining precision craftsmanship with durability.
